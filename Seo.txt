Q2.Identify 3 potential UX issues causing high bounce rates and suggest solutions

1. No Search  Option Problem: Users cannot quickly find cocktail recipes

2.No Filter Option on Recipe Page
If a user wants to find a cocktail recipe by ingredient like 
"lemon" or by difficulty like "beginner" but there is no filter option they have to manually scroll through all recipes on mobile

3.No Print or Save Recipe Option People who cook or make drinks at home want to 
save or print the recipe. If there is no easy save or bookmark option they leave to find the recipe somewhere else where they can save it easily.
